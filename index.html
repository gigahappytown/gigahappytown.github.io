Art is Red

People define art diferently. I believe that ART IS RED .
Art is often beautiful. Sometimes it contains meaning.
So what do I mean when I say that "Art is Red"?
The way I see it, there is only one criteria that makes something art.
Art takes something from the artist. This is always true. Art is red.
